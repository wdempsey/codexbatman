# Rubric: EDA Plan

## What Good Execution Looks Like

- Exploration is tied to framing objectives.
- Questions are prioritized and testable.
- Plot list is purposeful, not generic.
- Subgroup/confounder/outlier checks are explicit.
- Exit conditions to modeling are clear.

## Minimum Acceptable Outputs

- EDA plan document
- prioritized questions list
- plot list with rationale
- subgroup check plan
- confounder/outlier check plan

## Common Mistakes

- generic plot dumping
- missing subgroup analysis
- no confounder checks
- no outlier policy
- exploratory claims written as conclusions

## Review Checklist

- Are EDA questions linked to the decision context?
- Does every planned plot have a stated purpose?
- Are subgroup checks defined with practical thresholds?
- Are confounder and outlier checks operationalized?
- Is there a clear handoff condition to modeling?
