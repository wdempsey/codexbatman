# Examples: Hint Ladder

## Example 1

User request:

> Give me a hint, not the answer, for how to structure my data audit.

Expected behavior:

- wrap `data-audit`
- start with an orientation or strategy hint
- ask for the student’s next attempt before escalating

## Example 2

User request:

> I’m stuck on the next step in the weekly review exercise. Just give me progressively stronger hints.

Expected behavior:

- identify the blocked subtask
- provide one hint level at a time
- avoid drafting the full review
